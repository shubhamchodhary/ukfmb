The 5C’s Focus

Our 4 day immersive program will apprise you of situations and cases that address issues pertinent to control, career, conflict, capital & culture

Control

Governance. Members involved in the business Separating or Integration Business Ownership with management Creation of

Career

Growth. Avoiding nepotism, providing family members, with opportunities for advancement and rewards based on

Capital

Funding. Making it possible for family members to invest, reinvest or divest Attracting private equity Efficient

Conflict

Internal Interest. Addressing the conflicts among family members and/or between family and the business

Culture

Founder’s mentality. Disseminate the legacy of the founding members culture throughout the company
