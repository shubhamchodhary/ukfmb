Why Aartha

The Artha program is an immersive 4 day residential program focused on addressing the major pain points that family businesses face today. Co-developed with veterans from the industry, the program features key cases delivered from an Indian context. The aim is but simple, leave the participants with a toolkit that can catalyze the continued prosperity of their micro or small enterprises.

Location

Nestled in the villages of NITTE, under the guidance stalwarts from the industry - this one of a kind program is our attempt to offer the small and medium industries in India a sneak peak into what the future holds and aims to leave the participants with many questions meant to provoke thought and discussion.
