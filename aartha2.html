The 5C’s Focus

Our 4 day immersive program will apprise you of situations and cases that address issues pertinent to control, career, conflict, capital & culture

Control

Members involved in the business Separating or Integration Business Ownership with management.

Career

Avoiding nepotism, providing family members, with opportunities for advancement and rewards.

Capital

Making it possible for family members to invest, reinvest or divest Attracting private equity.

Conflict

Addressing the conflicts among family members and/or between family and the business.

Culture

Disseminate the legacy of the founding members culture throughout the company.

Location

Nestled in the villages of NITTE, under the guidance stalwarts from the industry - this one of a kind program is our attempt to offer the small and medium industries in India a sneak peak into what the future holds and aims to leave the participants with many questions meant to provoke thought and discussion.
